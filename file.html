About the Program

This BCA program is a collaborative effort by Navgurukul, Eternal University, and SBA, aimed at providing quality education and skills to students.The NavGurukul BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) program is an educational initiative aimed at providing underprivileged youth with the skills and knowledge required to pursue careers in technology. The program focuses on offering a comprehensive curriculum that includes computer science fundamentals, programming, and software development. NavGurukul emphasizes practical, hands-on learning and mentorship, ensuring students gain real-world experience. This initiative not only aims to bridge the education gap but also empowers students with the necessary tools to secure meaningful employment in the tech industry, thereby fostering social and economic mobility.
